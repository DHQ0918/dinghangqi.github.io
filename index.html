Research Interest

 I am a researcher in evolutionary computation and computer vision, especially evolutionary multitasking optimization and point cloud application. Currently, I focus on the following research topics:
  • Point Cloud Descriptor/Feature Optimization
  • Evolutionary Multitasking Optimization
  • Evolutionary Computation
  • Point Cloud Registration
